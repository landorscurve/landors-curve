How this works
You can add the same skill multiple times with different time periods. For example, if you taught part-time from 2005 to 2010 and then full-time from 2015 to 2023, add Teaching twice with different hours and years. We add all your hours together across every entry and calculate your true expertise level. It is not about one straight line — it is about the total hours you have invested over your lifetime. At 10,000 hours you reach Master level. Most people are surprised how much they actually know.
